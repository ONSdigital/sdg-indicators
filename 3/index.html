Goal 3 - Good Health and Well-Being

Ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages

Goal 3 aims to ensure the health and promote the well-being of everyone (of all ages) by: - improving reproductive, maternal and child health - ending epidemics of major contagious diseases - reducing non-contagious diseases - promoting mental health and well-being - reducing behavioural and environmental health-risk factors

Targets and indicators

By 2030, reduce the global maternal mortality ratio to less than 70 per 100,000 live births
